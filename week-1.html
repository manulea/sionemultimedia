Photoshop lab

This task was to combine a background image, and remove an image from it, to place it on to another background image.

This landscape portrait is “Argus”, from the game World of Warcraft.

This is St Clair beach.

This is Sunrize, my World of Warcraft character.

I think this is the moon… or a planet. I am so bad with shapes.

Here they are all combined…. I cropped out the St Clair image sky, and added the Argus background underneath the layer. Then I cropped out my Sunrize character and placed the layer on top of all the layers. Then to be sneaky, I added the cropped out moon, on top of the Argus picture.

This is the final image, where I placed the moon in between the two layers.
