The Main Storyboard

So the premise of the original story was based upon two fish waiting to be sold at a fish shop.

But the original premise wasn't a happy ending! Because there was only one fish at the end of the scene!

I was like hmm, that's not right, so I developed a new story which included the two fish to live a happy ending (in the movie)..

this is the outcome.

Two Fish

Description:

Fish one enters fish bowl.

Fish one swims around trying to get comfortable

Noises come from right of the screen

Red fish enters.

Red fish swims around

Fish one questions red fish's arrival

Red fish explains where he came from

Fish one explains where he came from

Both fish make a moment of friendship

Design techniques I used

I decided not to include narrative in the form of text because I tried to illustrate the fish's emotions by use of their body language and thought bubbles, and thought that it should be enough to convey (in terms of fish emotions) what it would be like to be an actual fish (if their memorys were longer than 3). The only thing that would be appropriate in terms of adding anything other than animations, would be to add the sound of a splash occuring during the fish as they enter the fish bowl. This would increase the illusion of the animation tremendously.

No real inspiration was the source of my ideas. Just a thought of using a fish as the subject for my animation, occured to me when thinking about what my star sign is. (pisces). It related to me so, I decided to make a fish animation.
