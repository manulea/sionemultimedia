Prologue

Starting shots of nice sunny day with Va’a ( Sione ), ‘pretending to talk with his friends’ as he goes back inside, away from the hot outdoors.

He opens the door, closes the door, while the camera follows him on his way to the computer.

He gets to the computer, and realizes.. Hey, I have an assignment due tomorrow!

[ This idea of having the assignment due tomorrow, is the basis of the Life Hack video ! ]

While reading out multiple life hacks from the internet, a thought process in the form of an action-replay, plays out in an almost (second reality) sort of thing, where the timeline of Sione speaking out the life hack for the first time, is accompanied by a video, demonstrating what the life hack actually is, is playing on top of the audio recording. ]

This then proceeds in Sione reading the second life hack out loud with the same sort of style where the video is on top of the audio recording, demonstrating the life hack that is being said out loud.

Sione will then say, hmm, nah I don’t really want to do that, implying that these demonstration videos haven’t been carried out yet and were just an idea from his head of what could’ve happened if he were to truly pursue the life hack. [ But in this reality, the life hack was just meant to show footage to the audience member in a sort of humorous but serious way ]

[ Next morning has come, and it’s Assignment day. Sione still hasn’t done anything ]

Genie will then come in and greet Sione, saying that Sione had failed in his timeline, and the Genie has come back in time to redeem Sione, so Genie then gives Sione the recording device that is used to pass the Multimedia assignment.

[ This video recording device is much better than the device that Sione was originally ‘thinking’ of recording the life hack videos, Now Sione can actually collect some ‘real’ HD footage ]

[ Now Sione is annotating the life hack in real life, but this time – with a better recording device ]

[ Life hack #1 – Clean your room in the morning ! ]

[ Life hack #2 – Spins off the life hack #1, why cleaning the mess is important! ]

Have shots of Sione cleaning the room

While an audio layer underneath is describing the life hack.

Have a shot where everything is within reach, to show to the audience the power of knowing where each item is!

Now Sione is ready to hand his assignment in!

With minutes to spare!
